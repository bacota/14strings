New Members

We sound better with more players, so new members are always welcome!

Some of our players have extensive experience with other instruments, some are members of other music groups, but a few of us had never played an instrument before joining 14Strings! If you can make time to practice, then you are welecome to play with us!

Come visit us at one of the practices or events on our calendar. If you're interested in learning to play a Filipino instrument, somebody will show you how to play and all you have to do is practice.

Members of the student group are provided with everything they need. Community members are encouraged to order their own instrument from the Phillipines, buy a tuner, and perhaps a music stand. The total cost is less than $300.

Cliff has also helped a few members build their own beautiful instruments!
